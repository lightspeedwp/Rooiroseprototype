## 1. Brand Overview

**Brand Name**: rooi rose (always lowercase)  
**Tagline**: verhale wat aanraak  
**English Translation**: "red roses" / "stories that matter"  
**Product Type**: Afrikaans lifestyle magazine (e-tydskrif)  
**Target Audience**: Afrikaans-speaking women aged 25-65, lifestyle-focused

### Brand Essence

rooi rose is an **Afrikaans lifestyle magazine** that celebrates the beauty of everyday life through authentic storytelling. We bring warmth, sophistication, and inspiration to our readers through carefully curated content about lifestyle, home, food, fashion, and personal stories.

**Brand Personality**:
- Warm and approachable
- Sophisticated but accessible
- Inspirational and aspirational
- Authentic and relatable
- Proudly Afrikaans

## 2. Visual Identity

### 2.1 Logo & Branding

**Logo Style**: Lowercase wordmark "rooi rose" in elegant serif typeface  
**Primary Usage**: Always lowercase (never "Rooi Rose" or "ROOI ROSE")  
**Logo Color**: #e01e12 (rooi rose red) on white backgrounds

**Logo Variants**:
1. **Primary**: Red wordmark on white
2. **Reverse**: White wordmark on red background
3. **Dark Mode**: Light pink (#ff4d42) wordmark on dark grey

**Protected Space**: Minimum clear space = height of lowercase "r" on all sides

**Minimum Size**:
- Digital: 120px wide
- Print: 30mm wide

### 2.3 Typography

#### Display Font: Playfair Display SC

**Usage**: H1, H2, category labels, pull quotes  
**Weights**: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-Bold)  
**Style**: Serif, small caps variant  
**Character**: Elegant, editorial, sophisticated

**Why Playfair Display SC?**
- Premium magazine aesthetic
- Small caps add refinement
- Excellent readability at large sizes
- Strong visual personality

**Typography Examples**:
```
LIFESTYLE  ← 14px Playfair Display SC, uppercase, +0.1em tracking
Verhale wat Aanraak  ← 48px Playfair Display SC, +0.05em tracking
```

---

#### Body Font: Karla

**Usage**: H3-H6, body text, UI elements  
**Weights**: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold)  
**Style**: Sans-serif, humanist  
**Character**: Clean, modern, readable

**Why Karla?**
- Optimized for long-form reading
- Warm, friendly personality
- Excellent legibility at small sizes
- Pairs beautifully with Playfair Display SC

**Typography Examples**:
```
Article body: 18px Karla Regular, 1.6 line-height
Headings: 22-28px Karla Semi-Bold
Captions: 12px Karla Regular
```

### 2.4 Photography Style

rooi rose photography is **authentic, warm, and aspirational** — showcasing real moments and beautiful living.

#### Photography Guidelines

**Style**:
- **Natural lighting** — Bright, airy, sun-filled
- **Warm tones** — Golden hour, warm color grading
- **Candid moments** — Authentic, unposed, relatable
- **Lifestyle focus** — Home, food, fashion, people

**Composition**:
- **Rule of thirds** — Balanced, professional
- **Generous negative space** — Room to breathe
- **Shallow depth of field** — Soft backgrounds, focused subjects
- **Eye-level perspective** — Intimate, personal

**Color Grading**:
- **Warm whites** — Creamy, not stark
- **Soft shadows** — Gentle, not harsh
- **Muted brights** — Sophisticated, not garish
- **Film-inspired** — Slight grain, vintage feel

**Subject Matter**:
- **People**: Natural smiles, genuine expressions, diverse ages
- **Food**: Rustic, homemade, appetizing, top-down or 45-degree angles
- **Interiors**: Cozy, lived-in, aspirational but achievable
- **Fashion**: Accessible, wearable, real bodies
- **Nature**: Gardens, flowers (especially roses!), outdoor living

---

#### Aspect Ratios

**Preferred Ratios**:
- **3:2** (landscape) — Featured images, hero sections (magazine standard)
- **4:5** (portrait) — Mobile hero, vertical stories
- **1:1** (square) — Gallery thumbnails, Instagram-style

**Avoid**:
- ❌ 16:9 (too cinematic)
- ❌ 4:3 (outdated)

---

#### Image Quality Standards

**Resolution**:
- **Hero images**: 1920x1280 (3:2) @ 72 DPI
- **Featured images**: 1200x800 (3:2) @ 72 DPI
- **Thumbnails**: 600x400 (3:2) @ 72 DPI

**File Format**:
- **Web**: JPEG (progressive) or WebP
- **Compression**: Maximum 85% quality (balance file size/quality)
- **File size**: < 200KB per image (hero < 500KB)

---

### 2.5 Iconography

**Style**: Simple, clean line icons with 2px stroke weight  
**Color**: Match text color (usually #222222 or #e01e12)  
**Size**: 20px, 24px, 32px, 40px (consistent scale)  
**Library**: Lucide React icons (https://lucide.dev)

**Usage Examples**:
- Category icons (Lifestyle, Food, Fashion, etc.)
- Social media icons (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, etc.)
- UI icons (Search, Menu, Close, Share, etc.)

**Icon Guidelines**:
- Use **outlined style** (not filled)
- Maintain **2px stroke weight** across all icons
- Icons should be **optically balanced** (not necessarily mathematically centered)
- Always include **accessible labels** for screen readers

## 3. Voice & Tone

### 3.1 Brand Voice

rooi rose speaks with a **warm, authentic, and inspiring** voice that makes readers feel welcomed, valued, and uplifted.

**Voice Attributes**:

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|:----------|:------------|:--------|
| **Warm** | Friendly, approachable, like a trusted friend | "Kom kuier saam ons..." (Come visit with us...) |
| **Authentic** | Genuine, honest, real stories from real people | First-person narratives, personal anecdotes |
| **Inspiring** | Uplifting, aspirational, motivating | "Jou eie storie is die mooiste een..." (Your own story is the most beautiful...) |
| **Sophisticated** | Polished, refined, well-crafted | Editorial language, thoughtful phrasing |
| **Proudly Afrikaans** | Celebrates Afrikaans language and culture | Afrikaans idioms, cultural references |

---

### 3.2 Writing Style

**Tone**:
- Conversational but polished
- Second-person ("jy", "jou") to create intimacy
- Short, digestible sentences (15-20 words average)
- Active voice preferred over passive

**Language**:
- **Afrikaans-first** — All content in Afrikaans
- **Regional sensitivity** — Inclusive of all South African regions
- **Accessible vocabulary** — Avoid overly academic language
- **Inclusive** — Gender-neutral where possible, diverse representation

**Formatting**:
- **Short paragraphs** — Maximum 3-4 sentences
- **Subheadings** — Break up long articles every 300-400 words
- **Pull quotes** — Highlight key insights
- **Lists** — Use bullet points for scanability

---

### 3.3 Headlines & Decks

**Headline Style**:
- **Clear and compelling** — What's the story about?
- **Emotional hook** — Why should readers care?
- **Active language** — Verbs over nouns
- **Length**: 5-10 words (50-70 characters)

**Headline Examples**:
✅ "Hoe een ma haar hartseer in kuns omskep het"  
✅ "Vyf eenvoudige resepte vir druk weekaande"  
✅ "Die tuin wat 'n gemeenskap saamgebring het"  
❌ "Interessante verhaal oor 'n vrou" (too vague)  
❌ "Die impak van sosiale media op moderne samelewings" (too academic)

**Deck/Summary Style**:
- **Expand on headline** — Add context, intrigue
- **Conversational** — Like telling a friend
- **Length**: 2-3 sentences (100-150 characters)
- **Active voice** — "Dis nie altyd maklik nie, maar..." vs. "Dit word nie altyd as maklik beskou nie..."

## 5. User Experience Principles

### 5.1 Editorial Design

**Magazine Aesthetic**:
- **Generous white space** — Room to breathe, not cluttered
- **Large, impactful images** — Photography-driven layouts
- **Clear typography hierarchy** — Easy to scan
- **Minimal navigation** — Content-first, chrome-second
- **Pull quotes** — Visual breaks, highlight key insights

**Layout Principles**:
- **12-column grid** — Flexible, modular layouts
- **3:2 aspect ratios** — Editorial standard
- **Generous padding** — 24-40px internal spacing
- **Section breaks** — 64-96px between major sections

---

### 5.2 Accessibility

rooi rose is committed to **WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance**:

**Color Contrast**:
- **Body text**: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast (#000000 on #FFFFFF = 21:1)
- **Large text**: Minimum 3:1 contrast (#e01e12 on #FFFFFF = 3.58:1 — AA Large)
- **Links**: Use #b51410 for AAA compliance (4.72:1)

**Typography**:
- **Minimum font size**: 16px body text
- **Line height**: 1.6 for long-form reading
- **Reading width**: Maximum 680px (45-75 characters per line)

**Navigation**:
- **Keyboard accessible** — All interactive elements
- **Focus indicators** — Visible, high-contrast
- **Screen reader friendly** — Semantic HTML, ARIA labels
- **Skip links** — Jump to main content

---

### 5.3 Performance

**Page Load Targets**:
- **First Contentful Paint**: < 1.5 seconds
- **Largest Contentful Paint**: < 2.5 seconds
- **Time to Interactive**: < 3.5 seconds

**Image Optimization**:
- **Lazy loading** — Below-the-fold images
- **Responsive images** — Srcset for different screen sizes
- **WebP format** — Modern image format with fallbacks
- **Compression**: 85% JPEG quality

## 8. Brand Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do

- Always write "rooi rose" in lowercase
- Use vibrant red (#e01e12) for brand accents
- Feature authentic, diverse photography
- Write in warm, conversational Afrikaans
- Prioritize generous white space
- Use Playfair Display SC for display headings
- Maintain 3:2 aspect ratios for featured images

### ❌ Don't

- Don't capitalize "Rooi Rose" or use "ROOI ROSE"
- Don't use rooi rose red (#e01e12) for body text links (use #b51410)
- Don't use stock photography that feels staged or fake
- Don't write in overly formal or academic language
- Don't clutter layouts — embrace white space
- Don't mix serif and sans-serif fonts outside of the system
- Don't use 16:9 or 4:3 aspect ratios
