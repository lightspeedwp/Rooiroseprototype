## Overview

This guideline defines the editorial content strategy for rooi rose, an Afrikaans lifestyle magazine. It establishes content focus areas, article types, editorial standards, and content planning frameworks to maintain a consistent, high-quality publication.

---

## 1. Brand Positioning

### Brand Promise

**rooi rose** is 'n tydskrif wat Afrikaanse vroue inspireer om hul beste lewe te leef — van kos en mode tot gesondheid en verhoudinge.

**Translation**: rooi rose is a magazine that inspires Afrikaans women to live their best lives — from food and fashion to health and relationships.

### Target Audience

**Primary Demographic**:
- Women aged 25-55
- Afrikaans-speaking (South Africa)
- Middle to upper-middle income
- Suburban/urban lifestyle
- Digitally savvy

**Psychographic Profile**:
- Values family and community
- Interested in self-improvement
- Enjoys entertaining and cooking
- Fashion-conscious but practical
- Health and wellness focused
- Seeks aspirational yet attainable content

### Editorial Voice

**Tone**: Warm, approachable, sophisticated  
**Style**: Conversational yet authoritative  
**Language**: Afrikaans (with occasional English terms where appropriate)

**Voice Characteristics**:
- **Empowering**: Content that helps readers feel capable
- **Authentic**: Real stories, real people, real advice
- **Aspirational**: Showcases the beautiful and achievable
- **Inclusive**: Welcoming to all women, regardless of background
- **Practical**: Actionable advice and tips

## 3. Article Types

### Feature Article (1,200-2,000 words)

**Purpose**: In-depth exploration of a topic  
**Structure**:
- Compelling headline
- Engaging introduction (hook)
- 3-5 main sections with subheadings
- Expert quotes or interviews
- Practical takeaways
- Conclusion with call-to-action

**Example**: "Die Wetenskaplike Gids tot Beter Slaap"

### How-To Guide (800-1,500 words)

**Purpose**: Step-by-step instructions  
**Structure**:
- Clear, action-oriented headline
- Brief introduction (why this matters)
- Numbered steps with images
- Tips and warnings (highlighted)
- Tools/materials list (if applicable)
- Conclusion with encouragement

**Example**: "Hoe om 'n Moestuintjie op Jou Balkon te Begin"

### Listicle (600-1,000 words)

**Purpose**: Quick, scannable content  
**Structure**:
- Numbered headline ("10 Wenke...")
- Brief introduction
- 5-15 items with subheadings
- 50-100 words per item
- Images or icons for visual interest
- Conclusion or bonus tip

**Example**: "15 Maklike Maandag-aandetes"

### Interview/Profile (1,000-1,800 words)

**Purpose**: Personal story or expert insight  
**Structure**:
- Engaging headline with name
- Introduction (who, why interesting)
- Background/context
- Q&A format or narrative
- Pull quotes (highlighted)
- Conclusion with takeaway

**Example**: "In Gesprek met Karen Zoid: Musiek, Moederskap en Meer"

### Recipe (400-600 words)

**Purpose**: Cooking instructions  
**Structure**:
- Descriptive headline
- Headnote (context, story)
- Prep/cook time, servings
- Ingredients list (metric)
- Step-by-step instructions
- Chef's notes/variations
- Nutritional info (optional)
- High-quality image

**Example**: "Ouma se Melktert met 'n Moderne Draai"

### Review (600-1,000 words)

**Purpose**: Product, book, or restaurant evaluation  
**Structure**:
- Clear headline (what's being reviewed)
- Quick verdict (rating, if applicable)
- Overview/context
- Detailed analysis (pros/cons)
- Comparison to alternatives
- Final recommendation
- Disclosure (if sponsored/gifted)

**Example**: "Resensie: Die Nuwe [Product Name]"

## 5. Editorial Standards

### Fact-Checking

- All claims must be verified with credible sources
- Medical/health content reviewed by qualified experts
- Recipes must be tested before publication
- Product reviews based on actual use (minimum 2 weeks)

### Sourcing

**Preferred Sources**:
- South African experts and organizations
- Peer-reviewed scientific journals
- Government health agencies
- Reputable international publications
- Original interviews

**Source Attribution**:
- Hyperlink to original sources
- Credit photographers and contributors
- Disclose sponsored content clearly

### Photography

**Guidelines**:
- High-resolution (minimum 1200px wide)
- Natural lighting preferred
- Authentic, unposed when possible
- Diverse representation
- Proper model releases for people
- Credit photographers

**Style**:
- Bright, airy aesthetic
- Minimal props
- Clean backgrounds
- Consistent color palette (aligned with brand)

### SEO Best Practices

**Headlines**:
- Include primary keyword
- 50-60 characters ideal
- Compelling and descriptive

**Meta Descriptions**:
- 150-160 characters
- Include target keyword
- Actionable language

**Internal Linking**:
- 3-5 internal links per article
- Link to related content
- Use descriptive anchor text

**Image Optimization**:
- Descriptive file names
- Alt text for accessibility
- Compressed for web (WebP format)

## 6. Content Metrics

### Success Indicators

**Engagement**:
- Page views
- Time on page (target: 2+ minutes)
- Scroll depth (target: 75%+)
- Social shares
- Comments

**SEO**:
- Organic search traffic
- Keyword rankings
- Backlinks
- Click-through rate

**Audience Growth**:
- Email newsletter signups
- Social media followers
- Returning visitors

### Performance Benchmarks

**Article Performance Tiers**:
- **Excellent**: 10,000+ views in first month
- **Good**: 5,000-10,000 views
- **Average**: 2,000-5,000 views
- **Underperforming**: <2,000 views

**Action Plan for Underperforming Content**:
- Review headline and meta description
- Update with fresh information
- Add more images or video
- Promote on social media
- Consider merging with related content

## 9. Ethical Guidelines

### Sponsored Content

**Disclosure**:
- Clearly label sponsored articles
- Maintain editorial independence
- Only partner with brands aligned with values

**Standards**:
- Honest reviews (positive and negative)
- No misleading claims
- Transparent about gifted products

### Diversity and Inclusion

**Representation**:
- Showcase diverse voices and perspectives
- Include people of all ages, races, body types
- Avoid stereotypes and assumptions
- Use inclusive language

### Privacy

**Reader Data**:
- Collect only necessary information
- Transparent privacy policy
- Secure data storage
- Option to opt-out

## 10. Crisis Management

### Content Corrections

**Minor Errors** (spelling, grammar):
- Correct immediately
- Note correction at bottom of article

**Factual Errors**:
- Correct with editor's note at top
- Update meta description updated date
- Notify readers who shared via email

**Major Issues** (legal, ethical):
- Retract article if necessary
- Publish apology/explanation
- Notify legal counsel

### Sensitive Topics

**Guidelines**:
- Handle with care and respect
- Consult experts before publishing
- Include trigger warnings if appropriate
- Provide resources for help

**Topics Requiring Extra Care**:
- Mental health
- Eating disorders
- Trauma and abuse
- Political or religious issues
