## Overview

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for creating, editing, and publishing content on the rooi rose magazine platform. Whether you're writing articles, creating recipes, or managing competitions, this guide ensures consistent, high-quality output.

Before publishing any content, verify:

- [ ] Headline follows SEO best practices (50-60 characters)
- [ ] Featured image uploaded (3:2 aspect ratio, minimum 1200x800px)
- [ ] Category and tags assigned

- [ ] Author byline correct
- [ ] Publish date and time set

## Part 1: Creating a Standard Article

### Step 1: Access the Editor

**WordPress**:
1. Log in to WordPress admin (`/wp-admin`)
2. Click **Posts** → **Add New**
3. You'll see the Block Editor (Gutenberg)

**What You'll See**:
- Title field at the top
- Content area (blocks)
- Sidebar with post settings (right)
- Toolbar with formatting options (top)

---

### Step 2: Write the Headline

**Best Practices**:
- **Length**: 50-60 characters (including spaces)
- **Format**: Actionable, specific, benefit-driven
- **Keyword**: Include primary keyword in first 5 words
- **Tone**: Warm, approachable, conversational

**Good Examples** ✅:
```
10 Maklike Weekaandete onder R100
Hoe om die Perfekte Melktert te Bak
5 Lente-neigings om Nou te Dra
```

**Avoid** ❌:
```
Weekaandete (too vague)
Sommige wenke vir kos wat jy kan probeer (too long, no benefit)
Klik hier vir resepte (clickbait)
```

**Character Counter**:
- Use built-in character counter in WordPress
- Or use online tool: https://charactercounter.com

---

### Step 3: Upload Featured Image

**Requirements**:
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:2 (magazine standard)
- **Minimum Size**: 1200x800px
- **Recommended Size**: 1800x1200px (for retina displays)
- **Format**: JPEG or WebP
- **File Size**: Under 500KB (optimized)

**How to Upload**:
1. In the sidebar, click **Featured Image**
2. Click **Set Featured Image**
3. Upload image or select from Media Library
4. Add **Alt Text** (describe image for accessibility)
   - Example: "Sappige hoenderborsies met vars kruie op 'n swart bord"
5. Click **Set Featured Image**

**Image Optimization Tips**:
- Use TinyPNG or Squoosh to compress before upload
- Crop to 3:2 aspect ratio before upload
- Use descriptive file names: `hoender-borsies-kruie.jpg` (not `IMG_1234.jpg`)

---

### Step 4: Write the Lead Paragraph

The first paragraph is critical — it hooks readers and appears in search results.

**Format**:
- **Font Size**: Medium (20px) — use "Lead" paragraph style
- **Length**: 2-3 sentences
- **Purpose**: Engage readers, summarize article

**Example**:
```
Dis Sondag-oggend. Die son skyn deur die kombuisvenster terwyl 
jy koffie maak en dink oor wat om vir middagete te kook. Hierdie 
10 maklike resepte sal jou lewe verander.
```

**How to Format**:
1. Type your lead paragraph
2. Select the text
3. In the toolbar, click the "Large text" style
4. Or use the block settings sidebar → Text size → Medium

---

### Step 5: Structure Your Article

Use headings to break up content and improve readability.

**Heading Hierarchy**:
- **H1**: Article title (automatically styled)
- **H2**: Major sections (e.g., "Bestanddele wat Saak Maak")
- **H3**: Subsections (e.g., "Voorbereiding")
- **H4**: Minor headings (e.g., "Wenke vir Beginners")

**How to Add Headings**:
1. Click the **+** button to add a new block
2. Search for "Heading"
3. Type your heading text
4. In the sidebar, select heading level (H2, H3, H4)

**Example Structure**:
```
# 10 Maklike Weekaandete onder R100 (H1 — automatic)

(Lead paragraph — large text)

## Hoekom Weekaandete Stressvol Is (H2)

(Body paragraphs...)

## Die 10 Resepte (H2)

### 1. Pasta Carbonara (H3)

(Recipe content...)

### 2. Roerbak-hoender (H3)

(Recipe content...)

## Wenke vir Sukses (H2)

(Conclusion...)
```

---

### Step 6: Format Body Text

**Paragraphs**:
- Keep short: 3-5 sentences maximum
- Use topic sentences
- Break up long paragraphs

**Lists**:
- Use bulleted lists for unordered items
- Use numbered lists for steps/instructions
- Add spacing between list items

**How to Add Lists**:
1. Click **+** → Search "List"
2. Choose "Bulleted" or "Numbered"
3. Type list items (press Enter for new item)

**Bold & Italic**:
- **Bold**: Important terms, emphasis
- *Italic*: Foreign words, book titles
- Select text → Click **B** (bold) or **I** (italic) in toolbar

**Links**:
- Select text to link
- Click link icon in toolbar
- Paste URL or search for internal page
- Choose "Open in new tab" for external links

**Example Paragraph**:
```
Wanneer dit by kos kom, is eenvoud dikwels die beste 
benadering. **Vars bestanddele**, minimale verwerking, 
en tyd om die geure te ontwikkel — dit is die geheim 
van great kookkuns.
```

---

### Step 7: Add Images

**In-Content Images**:
1. Click **+** → Search "Image"
2. Upload or select from library
3. Add alt text (required for accessibility)
4. Add caption (optional, but recommended)

**Image Best Practices**:
- Use 2-4 images per article (not too many)
- Place images strategically (after key sections)
- Always add alt text
- Keep captions short (1-2 sentences)

**Caption Format**:
```
Foto: [Photographer Name] | [Brief description]
```

**Example**:
```
Foto: Sarah van der Merwe | Die hoenderborsies kan 
vooraf voorberei word vir 'n vinniger aandete.
```

---

### Step 8: Add a Pull Quote

Pull quotes highlight key statements and create visual interest.

**When to Use**:
- Powerful quotes from interviews
- Key insights or statistics
- Memorable statements worth highlighting

**How to Add**:
1. Click **+** → Search "Quote"
2. Type or paste your quote
3. In the sidebar, select style: **Pull Quote**
4. Add citation (author/source) below quote

**Example**:
```
"Kos is liefde wat sigbaar gemaak word."
— Sarah van der Merwe, Kosskrywer
```

**Formatting Tips**:
- Keep quotes short (1-2 sentences max)
- Don't use pull quotes for every paragraph
- Place 1-2 pull quotes per article
- Position after important sections

---

### Step 9: Assign Category and Tags

**Categories** (Required):
- Choose **one primary category** (e.g., Kos, Mode, Skoonheid)
- Optionally add **one subcategory** (e.g., Kos → Resepte)

**How to Assign**:
1. In the sidebar, scroll to **Categories**
2. Check the appropriate box(es)
3. Don't select too many (1-2 max)

**Categories List**:
- Kos (Resepte, Bak, Aandete, etc.)
- Mode (Modeneigings, Modewenke, Inkopies)
- Skoonheid (Velversorging, Grimering, etc.)
- Gesondheid (Fiksheid, Voeding, Welstand)
- Bekendes
- Leefstyl (Huis en Tuin, Versiering, etc.)
- Jou lewe (Verhoudinge, Ouerskap, etc.)
- Ontspanning (Boeke, Flieks, TV-reekse, etc.)
- Wen (Competitions)
- Rooiwarm wenners

**Tags** (Optional):
- Add 3-5 relevant tags
- Use lowercase
- Separate with commas
- Examples: `melktert, resepte, bak, suidafrikaans`

---

### Step 10: Write Excerpt and Meta Description

**Excerpt** (Post Summary):
- Length: 150-160 characters
- Purpose: Appears on category pages, search results
- Include primary keyword
- Make it compelling (encourage clicks)

**How to Add**:
1. In the sidebar, scroll to **Excerpt**
2. Type your excerpt (150-160 characters)

**Example**:
```
Ontdek 10 maklike weekaandete wat minder as R100 kos. 
Van pasta tot roerbak-geregte — vinnige resepte vir 
besige gesinne.
```

**Meta Description** (SEO):
- If using Yoast SEO, scroll to **Yoast SEO** section
- Write meta description (same as excerpt is fine)
- Check SEO analysis for improvements

---

### Step 11: Set Author and Publish Date

**Author**:
1. In the sidebar, find **Author**
2. Select your name from dropdown
3. If byline differs, add custom byline field (ACF)

**Publish Date**:
1. In the sidebar, find **Publish**
2. Click **Immediately** (next to Publish button)
3. Set date and time
4. Click **OK**

**Best Publishing Times** (South African timezone):
- Morning: 6:00-9:00 AM (commute reading)
- Lunchtime: 12:00-1:00 PM (work break)
- Evening: 7:00-9:00 PM (leisure browsing)

---

### Step 12: Preview and Publish

**Preview**:
1. Click **Preview** button (top right)
2. Choose **Preview in new tab**
3. Check:
   - Headline displays correctly
   - Featured image shows
   - Text is readable (font, size, spacing)
   - Images load
   - Links work
   - Mobile responsive (resize browser)

**Publish**:
1. If preview looks good, click **Publish**
2. Confirm publish (click **Publish** again)
3. View live post (click **View Post**)

**Post-Publish Checklist**:
- [ ] Share on social media (Facebook, Instagram, X)
- [ ] Add to newsletter (if applicable)
- [ ] Monitor comments (respond within 24 hours)
- [ ] Check analytics (page views, engagement)

## Part 2: Creating a Recipe Article

Recipes have a specialized structure with ingredients, instructions, and metadata.

### Step 1: Use Recipe Template

**WordPress**:
1. Posts → Add New
2. Select **Recipe** post type (if available)
3. Or use standard post + ACF recipe fields

---

### Step 2: Recipe Title

**Format**: "[Dish Name] met 'n [Twist/Description]"

**Examples**:
```
Ouma se Melktert met 'n Moderne Draai
Vinnige Pasta Carbonara vir Weekaande
Slaai wat Jou Honger Stil
```

---

### Step 3: Recipe Headnote

**Purpose**: Set context, tell story, explain inspiration

**Length**: 2-3 sentences

**Example**:
```
Hierdie resep is 'n kombinasie van my ouma se 
oorspronklike melktert en 'n paar moderne wenke wat 
dit net bietjie makliker maak sonder om die 
tradisionele smaak te verloor.
```

---

### Step 4: Recipe Meta Fields

**Required Fields** (ACF):
- **Prep Time**: 20 minute
- **Cook Time**: 45 minute
- **Total Time**: 1 uur 5 minute
- **Servings**: 8-10
- **Difficulty**: Maklik, Medium, Moeilik

**How to Fill**:
1. Scroll to **Recipe Details** section (below editor)
2. Fill in each field
3. Use Afrikaans: "minute" not "minutes"

---

### Step 5: Ingredients List

**Format**: Bulleted list with metric measurements

**Structure**:
```
## Bestanddele

### Kors
- 250g koekmeel
- 125g koue botter
- 2 eetlepels suiker
- 1 eiergeel
- 2-3 eetlepels koue water

### Vulsel
- 500ml melk
- 3 eiergele
- 125ml suiker
- 30ml meel
- 15ml botter
- 5ml vanilla-ekstrak
- Kaneel vir strooi
```

**Tips**:
- Group by component (kors, vulsel, sous)
- Use metric (ml, g, kg)
- Cups/teaspoons acceptable for baking
- List in order of use

---

### Step 6: Instructions

**Format**: Numbered list with action verbs

**Structure**:
```
## Instruksies

1. **Maak die kors:** Meng meel, suiker en koue botter 
   totdat dit soos broodkrummels lyk.
2. **Voeg vog by:** Meng eiergeel en water, voeg by 
   meelmengsel.
3. **Rus:** Laat die deeg 30 minute in die yskas rus.
4. **Rol uit:** Rol deeg uit op 'n gemeelde oppervlak 
   tot 3mm dik.
5. **Bak:** Bak by 180°C vir 15 minute of tot 
   liggoud.
```

**Tips**:
- Start each step with bold action verb
- One action per step
- Include temperatures (°C)
- Include cooking times
- Be specific ("15 minute" not "until done")

---

### Step 7: Chef's Notes

**Purpose**: Tips, variations, substitutions, storage

**Format**: Bulleted list

**Example**:
```
## Sjef se Wenke

- Gebruik volroom melk vir die beste smaak
- Moenie die vulsel oorkook nie — dit verdik verder 
  terwyl dit afkoel
- Vars gemaalde kaneel maak 'n groot verskil
- Hou in yskas vir tot 3 dae
- Vries ongebakte kors vir tot 1 maand

## Variasies

- **Sjokolade melktert**: Voeg 50g kakao by vulsel
- **Sitrus melktert**: Voeg suurlemoensap en -skil by
- **Glutenvry**: Vervang meel met glutenvrye meel
```

---

### Step 8: Nutritional Information (Optional)

**Format**: Table or list

**Example**:
```
## Voedingswaarde (per sny)

Kalorieë: 285 | Vet: 12g | Koolhidrate: 38g | 
Proteïen: 6g
```

**How to Add**:
- Use online calculator (MyFitnessPal, Cronometer)
- Or skip if not critical
- Mark as "approximate"

## Part 4: SEO Optimization

### Yoast SEO Plugin

**Where**: Scroll to **Yoast SEO** section (below editor)

**Focus Keyphrase**:
1. Enter your primary keyword (e.g., "melktert resep")
2. Yoast analyzes your content
3. Follow suggestions to improve SEO score

**SEO Title**:
- Default: Your headline
- Edit if needed (keep under 60 characters)
- Include keyword

**Meta Description**:
- Same as excerpt (150-160 characters)
- Include keyword
- Make it compelling

**SEO Analysis**:
- Aim for **Green** score (good)
- Orange is acceptable
- Fix critical **Red** issues

**Common Issues**:
- ❌ Keyword not in first paragraph → Add keyword
- ❌ Meta description too long → Shorten to 160 chars
- ❌ No outbound links → Add 1-2 relevant external links
- ❌ No internal links → Add 3-5 links to related articles

---

### Readability Analysis

**Target**: Green or Orange score

**Common Issues**:
- ❌ Sentences too long → Shorten to 20 words max
- ❌ Paragraphs too long → Break into 3-5 sentences
- ❌ Passive voice → Use active voice
- ❌ No subheadings → Add H2/H3 headings

---

### Schema Markup

**Automatic** (via Yoast):
- Article schema (all posts)
- Organization schema (site-wide)

**Manual** (for recipes, FAQs):
- Use **FAQ block** for Q&A sections
- Use **Recipe schema** (if ACF configured)
- Verify with Google Rich Results Test

## Part 5: Image Best Practices

### Featured Images

**Composition**:
- Subject in focus, clear background
- Good lighting (natural preferred)
- 3:2 aspect ratio (crop in photo editor)

**Editing**:
- Brightness: Bright and airy (rooi rose aesthetic)
- Contrast: Medium (not too harsh)
- Saturation: Natural (don't oversaturate)

**Tools**:
- Canva (free, easy)
- Photoshop (advanced)
- Snapseed (mobile)

---

### Alt Text

**Purpose**: Accessibility for screen readers, SEO benefit

**Format**: Descriptive sentence (10-15 words)

**Good Examples** ✅:
```
Sappige hoenderborsies met vars kruie op 'n swart bord
Vrou wat groente sny in 'n moderne kombuis met natuurlike lig
Melktert sny met kaneel op 'n vintage bord met vurk
```

**Avoid** ❌:
```
food (too vague)
IMG_1234 (not descriptive)
chicken (missing context)
```

---

### Captions

**Purpose**: Provide context, credit photographer

**Format**: [Credit] | [Context/Description]

**Examples**:
```
Foto: Sarah van der Merwe | Die hoenderborsies kan 
vooraf voorberei word.

Foto: Unsplash | 'n Moderne kombuis met minimalistiese 
ontwerp.

Beeld: rooi rose Argief | Melktert is 'n Suid-
Afrikaanse gunsteling.
```

## Part 6: Proofreading Checklist

Before publishing, check:

### Content
- [ ] Headline compelling and SEO-optimized (50-60 chars)
- [ ] Lead paragraph hooks reader
- [ ] Article structure logical (intro, body, conclusion)
- [ ] Facts verified and sourced
- [ ] All claims accurate
- [ ] No plagiarism (original content)

### Style
- [ ] Consistent voice and tone (warm, approachable)
- [ ] Sentence variety (short and long)
- [ ] Paragraphs concise (3-5 sentences)
- [ ] No jargon or complex language
- [ ] Active voice used (not passive)

### Grammar
- [ ] Spelling checked (Afrikaans spell-checker)
- [ ] Punctuation correct (Oxford comma, em dashes)
- [ ] Capitalization consistent (sentence case)
- [ ] Numbers formatted correctly (words 1-10, numerals 11+)
- [ ] Measurements metric (unless recipe cups/teaspoons)

### Technical
- [ ] Featured image uploaded (3:2, min 1200x800px)
- [ ] Alt text on all images
- [ ] Category and tags assigned
- [ ] Excerpt written (150-160 chars)
- [ ] Meta description written
- [ ] Internal links added (3-5)
- [ ] External links work (open in new tab)
- [ ] Preview on mobile and desktop

### SEO
- [ ] Yoast SEO score Green or Orange
- [ ] Focus keyphrase in headline
- [ ] Focus keyphrase in first paragraph
- [ ] Meta description includes keyword
- [ ] URL slug clean and keyword-rich
- [ ] Schema markup present (automatic)

## Part 7: Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Too Many Categories

**Problem**: Confuses readers, dilutes SEO  
**Solution**: Choose 1-2 categories maximum

### 2. Missing Alt Text

**Problem**: Inaccessible to screen readers, SEO penalty  
**Solution**: Add alt text to EVERY image

### 3. Long Paragraphs

**Problem**: Hard to read, high bounce rate  
**Solution**: Keep paragraphs to 3-5 sentences

### 4. No Internal Links

**Problem**: Poor site navigation, lower engagement  
**Solution**: Add 3-5 internal links to related articles

### 5. Weak Headlines

**Problem**: Low click-through rates  
**Solution**: Use actionable, benefit-driven headlines

### 6. Skipping Preview

**Problem**: Formatting errors, broken links go live  
**Solution**: Always preview before publishing

### 7. Poor Image Quality

**Problem**: Unprofessional appearance  
**Solution**: Use high-resolution images (min 1200px wide)

### 8. No Excerpt

**Problem**: Ugly search result snippets  
**Solution**: Write custom excerpt (150-160 chars)

### 9. Publishing at Wrong Time

**Problem**: Lower initial traffic  
**Solution**: Publish at 6-9 AM, 12-1 PM, or 7-9 PM

### 10. Forgetting Social Share

**Problem**: Missed promotion opportunity  
**Solution**: Share on social media immediately after publishing

## Part 8: Quick Reference

### Afrikaans Style Guide

**Capitalization**:
- Sentence case for headings: "Die beste resepte vir winter"
- Capitalize proper nouns: Kaapstad, Karoo
- Lowercase seasons: winter, lente, somer, herfs
- Capitalize months/days: Januarie, Maandag

**Punctuation**:
- Oxford comma: Yes (kos, mode, en skoonheid)
- Quotation marks: Double quotes for speech
- Em dash: — (for emphasis, not --)
- Ellipsis: ... (three dots with space before)

**Numbers**:
- Words 1-10: twee eiers, vyf minute
- Numerals 11+: 15 minute, 250g
- Measurements: Always numerals (2 koppies, 180°C)

**Date Format**:
- DD Maand YYYY: 12 Maart 2026
- Time: 24-hour format (14:30)
