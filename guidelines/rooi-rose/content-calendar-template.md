## Part 4: Content Types

### Standard Article

**Length**: 800-1,200 words  
**Images**: 3-5 (featured + in-content)  
**Structure**: Intro, 3-5 sections (H2), conclusion  
**SEO**: 1 focus keyword, 3-5 tags  
**Frequency**: 3-4 per day

**Example Topics**:
- "10 Lente-modeneigings om Nou te Dra"
- "5 Wenke vir 'n Gesonder Leefstyl"
- "Die Beste Velversorging vir Winter"

---

### Recipe Article

**Length**: 500-800 words (+ recipe details)  
**Images**: 4-6 (featured, process, final dish)  
**Structure**: Headnote, ingredients, instructions, notes  
**SEO**: Recipe name as keyword  
**Frequency**: 1-2 per day

**Example Topics**:
- "Ouma se Tradisionele Melktert"
- "Vinnige Weekaand Pasta Carbonara"
- "Gesonde Slaai met Sitrusdressing"

---

### Listicle

**Length**: 600-1,000 words  
**Images**: 1 per list item (10-15 images total)  
**Structure**: Intro, numbered list (5-15 items), conclusion  
**SEO**: Number in keyword ("10 Wenke...")  
**Frequency**: 2-3 per week

**Example Topics**:
- "15 Moet-hê Modeitems vir Lente"
- "7 Maklike Grimeertrukke vir Beginners"
- "20 Tydbesparende Kombuis-hacks"

---

### How-To Guide

**Length**: 1,000-1,500 words  
**Images**: Step-by-step photos (8-12)  
**Structure**: Intro, materials, step-by-step, tips  
**SEO**: "Hoe om..." in keyword  
**Frequency**: 1-2 per week

**Example Topics**:
- "Hoe om Jou Eie Velskrub te Maak"
- "Stap-vir-Stap Gids: Perfekte Brood"
- "Hoe om 'n Klein Kombuis te Organiseer"

---

### Interview / Q&A

**Length**: 800-1,200 words  
**Images**: 3-5 (subject photos)  
**Structure**: Intro, Q&A format, conclusion  
**SEO**: Subject name in keyword  
**Frequency**: 1 per week

**Example Topics**:
- "In Gesprek met [Celebrity Name]"
- "5 Vrae met Jou Gunsteling Kok"
- "Ontmoet die Vrou agter [Brand]"

---

### Round-Up / Review

**Length**: 800-1,200 words  
**Images**: Product photos (5-10)  
**Structure**: Intro, product sections, verdict  
**SEO**: "Beste..." or "Top..." in keyword  
**Frequency**: 1 per week

**Example Topics**:
- "Die 10 Beste Moisturisers vir Winter"
- "Top 5 Boeke om Hierdie Maand te Lees"
- "Ons Gunsteling Keukenapparate van 2026"

---

### Seasonal / Trending

**Length**: 600-1,000 words  
**Images**: 3-5 (seasonal/trending visuals)  
**Structure**: Intro, trend breakdown, how-to apply  
**SEO**: Season/trend name in keyword  
**Frequency**: 2-3 per month

**Example Topics**:
- "5 Kersfeestradisies om te Begin"
- "Vroue se Dag: 10 Inspirerende Suid-Afrikaanse Vroue"
- "Die Grootste Modeneigings vir Lente 2026"

## Part 7: Newsletter Planning

### Newsletter Schedule

**Frequency**: Weekly (every Saturday at 8:00 AM)

**Structure**:
1. **Header**: rooi rose logo + tagline
2. **Intro**: Personal message from editor (50-100 words)
3. **Featured Article**: Hero image + headline + excerpt + CTA
4. **Top 5 Articles**: Small image + headline + link
5. **Quick Links**: Social media, website sections
6. **Footer**: Unsubscribe, contact info, privacy

---

### Newsletter Content Curation

**Selection Criteria**:
- ✅ Published in last 7 days
- ✅ High-performing (views, engagement)
- ✅ Category balance (not all Kos)
- ✅ Visual appeal (good images)
- ✅ Evergreen value (not time-sensitive)

**Weekly Curation Process** (Thursday 4:00 PM):
1. Review last 7 days' articles (Google Analytics)
2. Select top 5-7 articles (views + engagement)
3. Choose 1 featured article (best performer)
4. Write newsletter intro (100 words)
5. Design email (MailChimp)
6. Send test to team (Friday morning)
7. Schedule for Saturday 8:00 AM

---

### Newsletter Metrics

**Target KPIs**:
- Open rate: >20%
- Click-through rate: >3%
- Unsubscribe rate: <0.5%
- List growth: >100 subscribers/month

**A/B Testing**:
- Subject lines (2 versions)
- Send time (8 AM vs 10 AM)
- Featured article position
- CTA button text
