**Last updated**: 2026-03-01
**Folder**: patterns/card/
**Slug**: die-papier/card-sponsor-gold, gold-sponsor-silver, gold-sponsor-bronze

## Description
Tiered sponsor cards for gold, silver, and bronze sponsors.

## Implementation Notes
Different card sizes and border treatments for different tiers. Gold sponsors have largest cards with gold accents. Silver sponsors have mid-sized cards with silver accents. Bronze sponsors have smallest cards with bronze accents.
