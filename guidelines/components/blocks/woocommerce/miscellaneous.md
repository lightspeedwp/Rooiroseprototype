# WooCommerce Blocks: Miscellaneous

**Last updated**: 2026-03-03
**Category**: `woocommerce`

---

## Overview

Utility and speciality blocks that don't fit into other categories. Most are used sparingly or not at all in Die Papier.

---

## Block Inventory

### Cart Link
- **Name:** `woocommerce/cart-link`
- **Description:** Display a link to the cart page.
- **Supports:** color (background, link, text), spacing (padding), typography (fontSize)
- **Attributes:** `cartIcon`, `content`, `isPreview`
- **Pattern Usage:** Used in `woo-checkout.php` as "Terug na mandjie" breadcrumb link with `main-accent` colour.

### Catalog Sorting
- **Name:** `woocommerce/catalog-sorting`
- **Description:** Dropdown for changing product sort order.
- **Supports:** color (text, background), typography (fontSize)
- **Attributes:** `fontSize`, `useLabel`

### Classic Shortcode
- **Name:** `woocommerce/classic-shortcode`
- **Description:** Renders classic WooCommerce shortcodes (pre-block editor).
- **Supports:** align, interactivity (clientNavigation)
- **Attributes:** `align`, `shortcode`
- **Note:** Legacy block. Avoid for new patterns.

### Coming Soon
- **Name:** `woocommerce/coming-soon`
- **Description:** Coming soon / maintenance mode page content.
- **Supports:** color (background, text)
- **Attributes:** `color`, `comingSoonPatternId`, `storeOnly`

### Email Content
- **Name:** `woocommerce/email-content`
- **Description:** Placeholder block for WooCommerce email templates.
- **Supports:** email
- **Attributes:** `emailType`, `postId`

### Page Content Wrapper
- **Name:** `woocommerce/page-content-wrapper`
- **Description:** Wraps WooCommerce page content (cart, checkout, my-account).
- **Supports:** (none)
- **Attributes:** `page` (one of: "cart", "checkout", "order-received")
- **Pattern Usage:** Used in `woo-cart.php`, `woo-checkout.php`, `woo-order-confirmation.php`.

### Payment Method Icons
- **Name:** `woocommerce/payment-method-icons`
- **Description:** Display icons for available payment methods (Visa, MC, etc.).
- **Supports:** spacing (margin, padding)
- **Attributes:** `numberOfIcons`

### Single Product
- **Name:** `woocommerce/single-product`
- **Description:** Display a single product of your choice with full control.
- **Supports:** align (full, wide), interactivity
- **Attributes:** `isPreview`, `productId`

### Related Products
- **Name:** `woocommerce/related-products`
- **Description:** Display related products (auto-generated by WooCommerce).
- **Supports:** align, interactivity (clientNavigation)
