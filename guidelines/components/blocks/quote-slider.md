## Replacement Guide

Brand quotes are now rendered using the generic `dp/slider` block with `core/quote` InnerBlocks inside the `section-brand-quotes.php` pattern. This reuses the slider infrastructure and allows editors to add/remove/edit quotes directly in the block editor.

## Historical Reference

Standalone brand-quote carousel block. Auto-rotated through testimonial quotes with fade transition. Used on homepage and advertise page. Navy background with white text.
